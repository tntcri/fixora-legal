This Return and Refund Policy explains how Fixora handles refunds, cancellations, and disputes for transactions made through the Fixora mobile application. As a service marketplace, our policy is designed to be fair to both Buyers and Sellers.

Escrow Protection

All card payments on Fixora are held in escrow. Funds are only released to the Seller after the Buyer confirms successful delivery. This means you are protected — you never lose money for a service that was not delivered.

2. Refund Eligibility

A full refund is automatically processed in the following situations:

  • The Buyer cancels before the Seller accepts the booking
  • The Seller rejects the booking
  • The Buyer cancels an accepted booking before work begins
  • A dispute is resolved in the Buyer's favour by Fixora

Refunds are not issued in the following situations:

  • The Buyer has confirmed delivery and marked the booking as completed
  • A dispute is resolved in the Seller's favour by Fixora
  • Cash payments (Fixora does not mediate cash transactions)

3. Dispute Resolution

If you are dissatisfied with a delivered service, you may raise a dispute before confirming delivery:

  • Open the booking in the app → tap "Open Dispute"
  • Describe the issue clearly and attach evidence if available
  • Fixora will review the dispute within 3–5 business days
  • Both parties will be given an opportunity to respond
  • Fixora's decision on escrow fund release is final

4. Refund Processing Time

  • Escrow refunds for cancelled bookings: 3–7 business days to original payment method
  • Dispute-resolved refunds: 5–10 business days after decision
  • Refund timelines may vary depending on your bank or card issuer

5. Service Quality Complaints

If a service was completed but you are unsatisfied with the quality, please raise a dispute before confirming delivery. Once you tap "Accept Delivery" and the booking is marked Completed, the escrow funds are released and refunds cannot be issued. We strongly advise Buyers to inspect the work before confirming.

6. Seller Payouts

Sellers receive their payment (minus the 10% platform commission) after the Buyer confirms delivery and the booking is marked Completed. Payout timelines depend on the PayHere settlement schedule and your bank.

7. Force Majeure

In cases where a service cannot be delivered due to circumstances beyond either party's control (natural disasters, civil unrest, etc.), Fixora will process a full refund to the Buyer at its discretion.
