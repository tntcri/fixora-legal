Home Services Marketplace — Sri Lanka

Last updated: March 2026  |  Effective immediately upon account registration

Fixora ("we", "our", or "us") operates a home services marketplace mobile application in Sri Lanka. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Fixora app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Phone number — collected during registration and verified via OTP for authentication and transaction security
  • Name and display name — provided during signup to identify you to other users
  • Email address — collected if you register via email or Google Sign-In
  • Profile photo — optional, uploaded by you
  • Location data — city and approximate location used to show relevant service providers near you
  • Booking and transaction data — details of services booked, payment method, and transaction history
  • Device identifiers — used for push notifications (FCM) and fraud prevention
  • Usage data — browsing history within the app, search queries, and feature usage for improving our service

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Fixora account
  • To verify your identity via phone OTP before sensitive actions (payments, fund releases)
  • To facilitate bookings between buyers and service providers
  • To process payments securely via PayHere payment gateway
  • To send push notifications about your bookings, messages, and account activity
  • To provide customer support and resolve disputes
  • To improve our platform through analytics and performance monitoring
  • To comply with applicable Sri Lankan laws and regulations

3. Payment Information

Fixora uses PayHere, a licensed payment gateway in Sri Lanka, to process card payments. We do not store your card details on our servers. All payment transactions are processed and secured by PayHere in compliance with PCI-DSS standards. Funds for card payments are held in escrow and released to service providers only after successful service delivery and buyer confirmation.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Between buyers and sellers — name and booking details are shared to facilitate service delivery
  • PayHere — payment details shared for transaction processing
  • Firebase (Google) — we use Firebase for authentication, database, push notifications, and analytics
  • Legal requirements — if required by Sri Lankan law or court order

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Firebase servers located in the Asia Southeast region (Singapore). We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), Firebase App Check for API protection, and OTP verification for sensitive operations.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Booking and transaction records are retained for 7 years as required by Sri Lankan financial regulations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (note: this may affect your ability to use the platform)

8. Children's Privacy

Fixora is not intended for use by persons under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via push notification or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
